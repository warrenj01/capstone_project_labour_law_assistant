CN311/18 – Industrial Court (Criminal Side) 1/6 OSHI v Best Construct Co Ltd-Corrigendum 2023 IND 44 CN311/18 THE INTERMEDIATE COURT OF MAURITIUS (Criminal Side) In the matter of:- Occupational Safety And Health Inspectorate v/s Best Construct Co Ltd CORRIGENDUM The Heading in the present matter erroneously reads “THE INTERMEDIATE COURT OF MAURITIUS”, and should be read as “THE INDUSTRIAL COURT OF MAURITIUS” given the present matter was heard before the Industrial Court (Criminal Side).
