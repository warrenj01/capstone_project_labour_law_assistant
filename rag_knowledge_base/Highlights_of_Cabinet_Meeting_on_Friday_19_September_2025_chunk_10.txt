Cabinet has agreed to the promulgation of the Consumer Protection (Use of Liquefied Petroleum Gas in Small Cylinders) (Amendment No. 2) Regulations 2025 to provide visual distinction of domestic Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) Cylinders weighing 12kgs from commercial ones. An obligation will be imposed on all LPG suppliers and distributors to adopt clear and visible differences between domestic and commercial cylinders.
