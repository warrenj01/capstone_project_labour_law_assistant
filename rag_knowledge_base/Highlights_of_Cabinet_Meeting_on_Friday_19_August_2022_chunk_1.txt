Page 1 of 5 HIGHLIGHTS OF CABINET MEETING – FRIDAY 19 AUGUST 2022 1. Cabinet has agreed to amendments being brought to the Environment Protection (Banning of Plastic Bags) Regulations 2020 to – (a) provide for a moratorium, up to 31 August 2023, for compliance with Regulation 2, by virtue of which a biodegradable or compostable plastic bag should not contain petroleum-based materials or their derivatives; and (b) amend the list of standards for biodegradability and compostability of plastic bags
