CN763/18 – Industrial Court (Civil Side) 1/2 Munusami v Palmar-Corrigendum 2023 IND 7 CN763/18 THE INDUSTRIAL COURT OF MAURITIUS (Civil Side) In the matter of:- Mrs. Rajni MUNUSAMI Plaintiff v/s Palmar Limitée (In Receivership) Defendant CORRIGENDUM The Court’s attention has been drawn to the fact that there is an error in the computing of the amount of Severance Allowance at paragraph 2)b) in the Conclusion of the Judgment, as regards the amount itself, i.e. Rs12 410/- ought to have been used instead of Rs707 310/- and as to the mathematical calculations carried out in relation thereto. The amounts ought to read as follows: 2) b) a sum equal to one twelfth of the sum above multiplied by 02 months (i.e. 07-06-18 to 06-08-18) Rs12 410/- x 03 months x 2/12 = Rs6205/- Rs713 515/- (Rs144 491/-) (Doc. P2) CN763/18 – Industrial Court (Civil Side) 2/2 Rs581 434/- The amount of Severance Allowance on which the Defendant Company is to pay to the Plaintiff Interest is Rs569 024/- only. The amounts are amended accordingly. [Delivered by: D. Gayan, Ag. President] [Intermediate Court (Financial Crimes Division)] [Date: 26 January 2023]
