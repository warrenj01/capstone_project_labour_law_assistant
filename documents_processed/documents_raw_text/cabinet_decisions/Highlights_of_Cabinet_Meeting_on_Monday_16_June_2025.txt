Page 1 of 1 HIGHLIGHTS OF SPECIAL CABINET MEETING – MONDAY 16 JUNE 2025 Cabinet has taken note that in the context of the reform of the Pension System as announced in the Budget Speech 2025-2026, two committees have been set up as follows – (a) one under the chair of the Prime Minister to look into possible support to those persons aged between 60 and 65 years employed in sectors with difficult working conditions, and eventual means-tested eligible housewives/mothers; and (b) another one under the chair of the Minister of Social Integration, Social Security and National Solidarity to look into possible support to persons in a state of health-related disability to work. Cabinet has further taken note that the Prime Minister will make a Statement on the matter in the National Assembly on Tuesday 17 June 2025. ********
