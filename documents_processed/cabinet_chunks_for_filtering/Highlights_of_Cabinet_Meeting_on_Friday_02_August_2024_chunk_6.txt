Cabinet has agreed to the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding between the Rabindranath Tagore Institute, Mauritius, and the Rabindranath Tagore University (RNTU), Bhopal, India. The latter was established in 2010, named after India’s first Nobel Laureate poet, painter, and thinker, Gurudev Rabindranath Tagore.
