January 2025. *** 11. Cabinet has agreed to the Mauritius Institute of Education co-hosting the 5th World Curriculum Studies Conference together with the International Association for the Advancement of Curriculum Studies from 24 to 27 June 2025, in Mauritius. The theme of the conference would be “Curriculum Studies in the post Anthropocene Era” and would cover issues ranging from language in the curriculum, scientific education, artificial intelligence, early childhood and inclusive education
