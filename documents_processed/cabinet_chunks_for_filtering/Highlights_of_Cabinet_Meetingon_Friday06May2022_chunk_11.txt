The objectives of a mandatory wheat flour fortification programme, amongst others, are to - (a) reduce nutritional anaemia in female adolescents from 28.5 percent to 10 percent as specified in the National Plan of Action for Nutrition 2016-2020; and (b) achieve the global nutrition target of reducing anaemia by 50 percent by 2025.
