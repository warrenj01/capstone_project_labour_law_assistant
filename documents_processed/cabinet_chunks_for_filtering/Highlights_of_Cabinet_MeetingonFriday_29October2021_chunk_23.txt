18. Cabinet has taken note of the finalisation of the annual Health Statistics Report (HSR) for the year 2020 for the islands of Mauritius and Rodrigues by the Ministry of Health and Wellness. The HSR 2020 contains information on population and vital statistics, health infrastructure, health personnel, maternal and child health, notifiable diseases, private health institutions, morbidity, infant and child mortality, mortality (all ages), and other services as well as statistics on COVID-19.
