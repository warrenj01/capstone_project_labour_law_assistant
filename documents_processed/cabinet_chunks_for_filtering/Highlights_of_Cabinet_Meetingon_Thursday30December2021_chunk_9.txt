As at 29 December 2021, 949,894 persons had received a first dose of vaccine (representing 75 percent of the population). 910,890 persons were fully vaccinated (representing 72 percent of the population). 186,042 persons had received a booster dose (representing 14.7 percent of the population). Cabinet has further taken note that two repatriation flights have been effected to Rodrigues on 24 December 2021.
