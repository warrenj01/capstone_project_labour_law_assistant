Cabinet has also taken note that there were seven active cases in Rodrigues and all were asymptomatic. Cabinet has further taken note of progress in the COVID-19 Vaccination Programme, including the administration of the booster dose. As at 29 October 2021, 903,935 persons had received a first dose of vaccine (representing 71.3 percent of the population).
