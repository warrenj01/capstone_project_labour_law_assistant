Blue Economy and Fisheries and L’Institut de Recherche pour le Développement. The Déclaration d’intention pour la coopération sur l’économie bleue provides for mutual agreement and cooperation in the fields of, inter alia, campaigns at sea; marine biology and biotechnologies; marine genomics and biotechnology applications for aquaculture, pharmaceuticals and sea-derived products; detection of illegal, unreported and unregulated fishing; research in physical oceanography; and earth observation
