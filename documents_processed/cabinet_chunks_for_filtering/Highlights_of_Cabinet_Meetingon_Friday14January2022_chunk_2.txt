31 March 2022. *** 3. Cabinet has taken note of the situation of the COVID-19 pandemic prevailing across the world. Some 320.8 million cases have been reported globally, of which 264 million persons have been successfully treated. With regard to Mauritius, as at 12 January 2022, there were 745 active cases of COVID- 19, out of which 13 were admitted at the New ENT Hospital.
