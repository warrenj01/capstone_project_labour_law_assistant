Cabinet has further taken note of progress in the COVID-19 Vaccination Programme, including the administration of the booster dose in the Republic of Mauritius. As at 11 November 2021, 909,824 persons had received a first dose of vaccine (representing 71.9 percent of the population). 871,910 persons had been fully vaccinated
