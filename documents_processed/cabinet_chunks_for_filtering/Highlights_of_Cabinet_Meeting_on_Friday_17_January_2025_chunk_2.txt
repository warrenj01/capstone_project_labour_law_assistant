The CWA would implement a multi-pronged Communication Plan so as to inform and sensitise the general public on the critical water situation, the need to conserve water and tips on how to save water in daily tasks/activities. The Plan would include broadcast of short clips on radios, television and social media and regular interventions in the press by officers of the Water Resources Commission and the CWA.
