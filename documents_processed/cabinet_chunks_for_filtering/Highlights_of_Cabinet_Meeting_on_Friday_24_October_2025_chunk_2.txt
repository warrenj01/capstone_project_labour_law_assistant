Code (Amendment No. 2) Bill into the National Assembly. The United Kingdom and Mauritius have, in May 2025, signed a treaty relating to the Chagos Archipelago in which Mauritius is unequivocally recognised as being sovereign over the Chagos Archipelago. Accordingly, the object of the Bill is to repeal section 76B of the Criminal Code whereby the wrongful acts of misrepresenting the sovereignty of Mauritius by producing, distributing, supply or marketing any coin, stamp, official map or other
