As at 21 April 2022, 1,007,364 persons had received a first dose of vaccine (representing 79.6 percent of the population). 973,303 persons were fully vaccinated (representing 76.9 percent of population). 620,724 persons had received a first booster dose (representing 49 percent of the population). 2,166 persons had received a second booster dose.
