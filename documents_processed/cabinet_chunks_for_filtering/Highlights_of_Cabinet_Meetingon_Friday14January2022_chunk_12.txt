29 January 2022. *** 12. Cabinet has taken note that restrictions were imposed on the movement of livestock from Rodrigues to Mauritius since the outbreak of Foot and Mouth Disease in the island on 11 March 2021. A protocol, outlining a series of animal health and traceability conditions that have to be complied with, has been worked out in order to ease the movement of animals from Rodrigues.
