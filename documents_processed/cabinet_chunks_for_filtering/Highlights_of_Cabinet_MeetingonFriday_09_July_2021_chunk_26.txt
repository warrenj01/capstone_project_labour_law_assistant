With regard to Mauritius, as from March 2020 to 09 July 2021, 2,163 cases (including imported cases) of COVID-19 had been registered. There were 371 active cases of COVID-19 in Mauritius, out of which 342 were local cases and 29 imported cases. Contact tracing exercises and random testing are being carried out in accordance with the established protocol.
