The application which is a fundamental tool, would allow the public at large and stakeholders to compare prices of products and make an intelligent purchasing decision. Consumers would have access to the retail prices of a basket of 100 commodities of more than 450 brands being disseminated on MOPRI. The defined basket of commodities, would be reviewed on a regular basis. The mobile application is available on the website https://mopri.govmu.org/
