Cabinet has taken note that the University of Mauritius would organise the 2022 Society of Transnational Academic Researchers (STAR) Global Conference from 07 to 08 December 2022. The international conference is being co-organised with the STAR Scholar Network, in collaboration with the Economic Development Board and the Mauritius Chamber of Commerce and Industry and would be in hybrid mode.
