*** 4. Cabinet has agreed to the holding of the 14th World Tamil Teachers’ Conference from 08 to 10 December 2023 in Mauritius. The Conference is being organised by the International Association for Tamil Teaching and Learning, in collaboration with the Primary School Tamil Teachers’ Union, the Mahatma Gandhi Institute and the Ministry of Education, Tertiary Education, Science and Technology. The Conference would have as overriding theme “Highlighting the Spoken Tamil in Teaching and Learning”.
