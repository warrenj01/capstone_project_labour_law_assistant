All-In-One Travel Form is available online to facilitate the current travel formalities for incoming passengers to Mauritius. The passengers would be able to fill in the Form prior to departure and would receive an email with a document comprising the Disembarkation Card, the Health Declaration Forms and the Health COVID-19 Laboratory Request Form. The Form is currently available on the website of the MTPA, Air Mauritius and ATOL and would be available on other websites including airlines. ***
