Out of 12,527 school candidates who took part in the 2024 School Certificate examinations, 9,101 were successful, resulting in a percentage pass rate of 72.65% compared to 73.71% for 2023. Seven secondary schools secured a 100% pass rate and 633 school candidates scored an aggregate of 10 or below, of which 280 were boys and 353 girls. 142 candidates obtained a grade aggregate of 6.
