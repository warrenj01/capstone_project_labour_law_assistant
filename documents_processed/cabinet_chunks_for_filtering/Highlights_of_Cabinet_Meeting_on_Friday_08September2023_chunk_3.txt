Cabinet has taken note of the performance of Mauritian athletes in the 11th edition of the Indian Ocean Islands Games (IOIG) held from 25 August to 03 September 2023 in Madagascar. The Mauritian team secured a total of 281 medals compared to 224 medals in the IOIG 2019. The athletes won 91 gold medals, 87 silver medals, and 103 bronze medals.
