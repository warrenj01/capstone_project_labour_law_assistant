Bengaluru Skill Summit 2025 held in Bengaluru, India. The Summit brought together stakeholders from across the skilling ecosystem including Government leaders, industry experts, global organisations, educators, training providers, and youth, to develop and drive actionable strategies for reskilling, upskilling, digital transformation, and employability in emerging sectors. The Minister participated in a Roundtable session on Global Best Practices in Skilling and delivered a key-note address.
