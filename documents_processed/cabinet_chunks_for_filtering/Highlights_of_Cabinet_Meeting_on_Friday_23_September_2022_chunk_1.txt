Page 1 of 4 HIGHLIGHTS OF CABINET MEETING – FRIDAY 23 SEPTEMBER 2022 1. Cabinet has agreed to the accession of the Republic of Mauritius to the Patent Cooperation Treaty (PCT). The Treaty which is administered by the World Intellectual Property Organization, makes it possible to seek patent protection for an invention simultaneously in a large number of countries by filing an “international” application. Such an application may be filed by anyone who is a national or resident of a PCT
