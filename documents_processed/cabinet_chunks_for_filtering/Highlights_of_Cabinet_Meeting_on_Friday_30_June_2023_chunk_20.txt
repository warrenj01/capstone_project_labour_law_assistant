The ILC endorsed the abrogation of one Convention, the withdrawal of four Conventions, one Protocol and 18 Recommendations, as these instruments were considered to have lost their purpose and have become outdated. The Conference also approved the report of the “Committee on the Application of Standards”, which is a core supervisory body of the ILO’s standards system. ***
