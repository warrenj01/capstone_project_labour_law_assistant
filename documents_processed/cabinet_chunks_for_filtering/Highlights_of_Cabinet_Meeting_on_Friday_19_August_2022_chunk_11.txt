Cabinet has taken note of the events which would be organised by the Ministry of Social Integration, Social Security and National Solidarity in the context of the International Day of Older Persons on Saturday 01 October 2022. The theme chosen by the United Nations for this Day is “The Resilience and Contributions of Older Women”.
