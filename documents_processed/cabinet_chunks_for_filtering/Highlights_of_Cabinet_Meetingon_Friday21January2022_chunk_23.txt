Regional Integration and International Trade had with HE Mr Nasser Bourita, Minister of Foreign Affairs, African Cooperation and Moroccan Expatriates of the Kingdom of Morocco. The main issues discussed during the meeting were - (a) the good bilateral relations which are based on common values such as respect for democracy, good governance and human rights; (b) sharing of information about the measures being taken in the respective countries to mitigate the adverse effects of the COVID-19
