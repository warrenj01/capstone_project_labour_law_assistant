The objectives of the ninth Special Session of AMCEN were to, inter alia, consider matters relating to the Resumed fifth Session of the United Nations Environment Assembly (UNEA) scheduled from 28 February to 02 March 2022, in Kenya and agree on Africa’s common position for engagement at the Assembly, adopt Africa’s resolutions on green recovery, circular economy, biodiversity and health and plastic pollution, nominate a Member State as incoming President
