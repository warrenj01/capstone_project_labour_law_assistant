The UK-ESA EPA provides, inter alia, for an automatic derogation of 6,300 tons of preserved tuna and 340 tons of tuna loins annually, normally renewable every five years by the UK-ESA EPA Committee. This derogation is shared among the ESA tuna- exporting States, namely Mauritius, Seychelles, and Madagascar. The derogation allows the sourcing of raw tuna from third countries
