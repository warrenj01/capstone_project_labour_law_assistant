Page 7 of 7 Cabinet has also taken note of progress in the COVID-19 Vaccination Programme, including the administration of the booster dose in the Republic of Mauritius. As at 20 January 2022, 964,309 persons had received a first dose of vaccine (representing 76.2 percent of the population). 927,168 persons were fully vaccinated (representing 73.2 percent of the population).
