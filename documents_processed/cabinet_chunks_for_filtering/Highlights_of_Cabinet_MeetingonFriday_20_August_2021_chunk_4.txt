Cabinet has taken note that the Sanitary and Phytosanitary Information Management System Portal (SPS) would be launched by the Ministry of Agro-Industry and Food Security on 23 August 2021. The Portal would be operated by the National Plant Protection Office and the Livestock and Veterinary Division of that Ministry. The purpose of the SPS Portal is to provide a platform to traders in order to obtain, from one single source, all the SPS related information. Traders
