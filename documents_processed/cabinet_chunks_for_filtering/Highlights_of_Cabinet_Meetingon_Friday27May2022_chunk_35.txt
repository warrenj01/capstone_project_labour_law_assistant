Cabinet has taken note that election for the position of Executive Directors on the Board of Governors of the African Development Bank Group was held on 26 May 2022. Mr Gerard Pascal Bussier, Deputy Financial Secretary at the Ministry of Finance, Economic Planning and Development, the nominated Mauritian candidate for the post, has been elected as one of the Executive Directors. He would hold the post for the next three years, starting as from 01 August
