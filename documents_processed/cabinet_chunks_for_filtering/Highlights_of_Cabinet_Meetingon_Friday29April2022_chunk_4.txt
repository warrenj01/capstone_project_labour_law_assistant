The laying of the foundation stone of the WHO-Global Centre for Traditional Medicine was held on 19 April 2022 in Jamnagar, Gujarat, by Prime Minister Modi in the presence of Dr Tedros Ghebreyesus, Director-General of WHO, and the Prime Minister of Mauritius.
