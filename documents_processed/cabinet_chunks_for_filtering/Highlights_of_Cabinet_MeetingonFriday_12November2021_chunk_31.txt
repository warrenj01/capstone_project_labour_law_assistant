Five varieties of local eucalyptus, 11 varieties of imported eucalyptus from Australia and one variety of Beema Bamboo from India have been planted for experiment at Belle Rive. Similar trial sites have been established at Valton, Antoinette, Mon Desert Alma, Bel Etang and Sans Souci.
