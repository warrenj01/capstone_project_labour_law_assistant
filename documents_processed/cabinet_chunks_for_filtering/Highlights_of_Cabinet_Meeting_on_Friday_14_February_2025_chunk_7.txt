School Certificate (HSC) Examinations 2024. Out of a total of 7,425 school candidates who took part in the 2024 HSC Examinations in the Republic of Mauritius, 5,857 were successful resulting in a percentage pass rate of 78.88%, compared to 84.40% in 2023. 1,681 school candidates have scored at least one “A” at Principal Level in 2024, compared to 1,839 in 2023.
