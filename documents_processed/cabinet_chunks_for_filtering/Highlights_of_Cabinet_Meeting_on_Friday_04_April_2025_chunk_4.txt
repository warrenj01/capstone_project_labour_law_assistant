Vallée d’Osterlog Endemic Garden Foundation Act. The National Parks and Conservation Service would, under the Native Terrestrial Biodiversity and National Parks Act, take over the functions and powers of the Vallée d’Osterlog Endemic Garden Foundation and the Vallée d’Osterlog Endemic Garden would henceforth be managed, administered, conserved, maintained and developed by the National Parks and Conservation Service.
