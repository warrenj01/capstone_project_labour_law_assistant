Cabinet has taken note of the implementation, as from 10 September 2024, of the National Colorectal Cancer Screening Programme by the Ministry of Health and Wellness. The National Colorectal Cancer Screening Programme is designed to target all Mauritians attaining 60 years of age and is in the first phase, being rolled out in the Port Louis region.
