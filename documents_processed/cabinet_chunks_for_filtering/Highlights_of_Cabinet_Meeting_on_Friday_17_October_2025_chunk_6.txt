2. Cabinet has agreed to the introduction of the Court of Rodrigues Jurisdiction (Amendment) Bill into the National Assembly. The object of the Bill is to amend the Court of Rodrigues Jurisdiction Act with a view to extending the application of the Children’s Court Act 2020 to Rodrigues.
