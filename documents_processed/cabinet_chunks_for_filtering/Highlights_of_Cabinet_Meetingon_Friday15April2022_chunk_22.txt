Shavana Haythornthwaite, Adviser and Head of the Human Rights Unit of the Commonwealth Secretariat in London would be in Mauritius from 23 to 28 May 2022 for the holding of two workshops in collaboration with the Human Rights Division of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Regional Integration and International Trade. The themes of the workshops are ‘Human Rights and Youth Engagement’ and ‘Business and Human Rights’. The workshop on ‘Human Rights and Youth Engagement’ would be held from 23 to 25
