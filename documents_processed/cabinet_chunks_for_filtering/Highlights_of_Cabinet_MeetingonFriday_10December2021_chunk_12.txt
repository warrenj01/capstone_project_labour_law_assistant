One of the first areas of cooperation under the Memorandum of Understanding would be the collaboration of the MOI with the IOC on the ongoing regional ECOFISH programme. The overall objective of the ECOFISH programme is to enhance equitable economic growth by promoting sustainable fisheries in the East Africa-South Africa-Indian Ocean
