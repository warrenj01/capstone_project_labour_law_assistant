Research at the University of Mauritius from 01 to 05 September 2025. POLY-CHAR is a non-profit and non-governmental organisation composed of a five-member Executive Committee and a 40-member scientific committee from across the globe. POLY-CHAR, rooted in polymer synthesis, characterisation, processing, application, recycling, education and its environmental/social consequences plays an important role in unifying polymer scientists across the world.
