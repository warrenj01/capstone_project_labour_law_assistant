Cabinet has taken note of the activities being organised by the Seafarers’ Welfare Fund on Monday 26 June 2023 to mark the celebration of the “Day of the Seafarer”. The “Day of the Seafarer” is observed every year on 25 June and aims to increase awareness among the general public of the unique and vital role of seafarer in the economy trade and regular civil life
