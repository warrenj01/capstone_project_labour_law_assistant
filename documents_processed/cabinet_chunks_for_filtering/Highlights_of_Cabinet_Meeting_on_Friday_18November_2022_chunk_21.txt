The White Grub Protocol was signed between the French and Mauritian Authorities in 1990. During the implementation of this Protocol, control is exercised on all movement of planes and ship vessels between Mauritius and Reunion Island. The Protocol is time- bound, i.e. between 18 30 hours to 20 30 hours whereby flights are not allowed to land and ships are not allowed
