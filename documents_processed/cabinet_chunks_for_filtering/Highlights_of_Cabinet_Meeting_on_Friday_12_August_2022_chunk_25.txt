Cabinet has taken note that Mauritius would host the 38th Annual General Meeting of the Board of Governors of the Eastern and Southern African Trade and Development Bank Group (TDB), on 24 and 25 August 2022. Mauritius would be assuming the role of Chairperson of the Board of Governors, in line with the Board of Governors' Rules of Procedure and the provisions of the TDB Charter.
